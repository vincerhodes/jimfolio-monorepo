Ordinary Script
京 · The Character
In oracle bone script, 京 is drawn as a tall watchtower rising above a raised earthen platform — the kind of grand elevated structure that marked a royal settlement. The pointed roof soars above a rectangular body on a stepped foundation. From this image of a commanding, visible-from-afar building came the meaning great capital, important place. 北京 literally means "Northern Capital."

The most dramatic stretch of the Wall near Beijing — wild crenellations across forested ridgelines, reached by cable car. The least crowded of the main sections.

🚡Cable car available — easy on the knees

Xi'an
Ancient Silk Road Capital · 丝绸之路
甲骨文
Oracle Bone
→
楷書
Ordinary Script
安 · The Character
The oracle bone form of 安 is a picture of a woman seated quietly beneath a roof — the character for woman (女) sheltered inside the character for a dwelling (宀). A woman safe under her own roof was the image of peace and harmony. From this came the meanings peaceful, safe, content, settled. 西安 means "Western Peace" — China's capital for over a thousand years.

UNESCO
⚔️
秦始皇兵马俑
Terracotta Warriors

Eight thousand life-size clay soldiers buried in 210 BC to guard the First Emperor. Discovered in 1974 by a farmer digging a well. Every single face is unique.

🗓️Built 246–208 BC under Qin Shi Huang
⏱️Open 08:30 · allow 2–3 hours
🦶Flat hangar halls — good for knees
XA · 02
🍢
回民街
Muslim Quarter

Lantern-hung alleys where Xi'an's Hui Muslim community has traded for a thousand years. Lamb skewers, pomegranate juice, cumin smoke — a teenager's dream of street food.

🕌Home to the Great Mosque, 742 AD
🌙Most atmospheric at dusk, lanterns lit
XA · 03
🔔
钟楼 / 大雁塔
Bell Tower & Big Wild Goose Pagoda

The Bell Tower marks the exact centre of ancient Xi'an. The Pagoda was built in 652 AD to house Buddhist scriptures carried back from India along the Silk Road by the monk Xuanzang.

🏗️Bell Tower 1384 AD · Pagoda 652 AD
⛲Musical fountain show at night in the square

📚

都江堰
Dujiangyan
Ancient Engineering · UNESCO · 世界遗产
甲骨文
Oracle Bone
→
楷書
Ordinary Script
川 · The Character
川 in oracle bone script is pure picture: three flowing lines side by side, each curving to suggest current. Drawings of water channels — not one river but branching flows, exactly like Li Bing's irrigation system here. 川 means "river, flowing waterway," and gives Sichuan (四川 — Four Rivers) its name.
DJ · 01
UNESCO
💧
都江堰水利工程
Dujiangyan Irrigation System

The world's oldest operating irrigation system — built in 256 BC without a dam. The river splits itself and regulates itself using only geometry. Still watering 5.3 million hectares today.

🗓️Built 256 BC by governor Li Bing · still working
🌊No dam, no valves — the river regulates itself
🚶Mostly flat riverside paths

四川 · Four Rivers · The Province
Chengdu sits in the heart of 四川 — four rivers. The Min, the Tuo, the Jialing, and the Wu flow through the Red Basin, making this one of the most fertile regions in China. Li Bing's irrigation at Dujiangyan made it all possible. When you see a panda eating bamboo in the reserve, you are looking at the landscape those managed waters created.

Wolong Giant Panda Reserve

A real mountain nature reserve, not a zoo. Pandas are most active 9–11:30am, tumbling around with spectacular indifference to onlookers. Buggy service available inside.

🐾Active time: 09:00–11:30 only — arrive early

CD · 02
⚔️
武侯祠
Wuhou Temple

A temple-museum for the heroes of the Three Kingdoms (220–280 AD). The great strategist Zhuge Liang is enshrined here — China's eternal symbol of wisdom and loyalty. Shaded gardens, gentle walking, many benches.

🏛️Founded 223 AD · rebuilt 304 AD
🍜Adjacent to Jinli Old Street for food

🏡
杜甫草堂
Du Fu's Thatched Cottage

Where the Tang poet Du Fu lived in exile (759–762 AD), writing 240 of his 1,400 poems. A serene garden with lotus ponds and bamboo groves. The most knee-friendly site on the whole trip.

✍️Du Fu wrote here during the An Lushan Rebellion
🍵Traditional teahouses within the grounds

巫山 · 三峡
Wushan & the Three Gorges
Yangtze River · The Gorges · 长江三峡
甲骨文
Oracle Bone
→
楷書
Ordinary Script
巫 · The Character
The oracle bone form of 巫 is a vivid picture: a shaman standing with arms spread wide, a ritual object held in each hand, caught mid-dance while calling spirits. 巫山 means Shaman Mountain — named not for its scenery but for what ancient peoples felt standing inside the gorge: the presence of something other than ordinary life.
WS · 01
🚢
小三峡游船
Little Three Gorges Cruise

A four-hour boat journey into the Daning River — sheer limestone cliffs hundreds of metres on both sides, wild macaque monkeys on the banks. Seated throughout. The most spectacular thing on the whole itinerary.

⛵Departures 09:00 and 14:00 from Wushan
♿Seated throughout — ideal for mobility
🦅Macaque monkeys on the banks, river eagles above
WS · 02
🏔️
神女峰
Shennv Peak — The Goddess

A solitary rock pinnacle with the unmistakable silhouette of a woman — Yao Ji, daughter of the Queen Mother of the West, who turned to stone rather than leave the mountains she loved. Shuttle buses to viewpoint.

🚌Shuttle buses — no difficult walking needed
☁️Most dramatic in morning mist
WS · 03
🏯
白帝城
Baidicheng — White Emperor City

A temple-fortress on a rocky island at the mouth of Qutang Gorge. The poet Li Bai received his pardon from exile here at dawn in 762 AD and wrote one of China's most memorised poems. Views down the gorge are unforgettable.

📜Li Bai's 早发白帝城 written here, 762 AD
🏝️On a rocky island — reached by boat or bridge
📍At Fengjie — near your car pick-up point
金文
Bronze Ware Script
→
楷書
Ordinary Script
江 · The Great River · and the Ganges
江 is a phono-semantic compound: the water radical (氵, three flowing drops) on the left combined with 工 (gōng) which carries the sound. 江 specifically meant the great river of the south — the Yangtze — as distinct from 河 (hé), the Yellow River of the north. Remarkably, the Old Chinese pronunciation of 江 (krong / klong) is strikingly close to river names across Southeast Asia: Sanskrit Gaṅgā (the Ganges), Khmer Mekong (the great river), and the -kong root in many river names. The oldest sound for "great river in the south" may share a common origin older than any single civilisation.

重庆
Chongqing
Mountain Megalopolis · 山城
甲骨文
Oracle Bone
→
楷書
Ordinary Script
庆 · The Character
In oracle bone script, 庆 pictures a person wearing a deer skin on their back, with the character for heart (心) beneath. The deer skin was a precious ceremonial gift — to arrive wearing one was to come bearing the most heartfelt congratulations. From this vivid image of celebration came the meaning to celebrate from the heart. 重庆 means "double celebration."
CQ · 01
🏮
洪崖洞
Hongya Cave

Eleven stacked floors of restaurants and bars built into a cliff face over the Jialing River, blazing with red lanterns at night. Said to have inspired Miyazaki's Spirited Away. Arrive at dusk.

🌆Best at dusk — arrive around 6pm
⚠️Multiple levels involve stairs — plan the route carefully
CQ · 02
🗽

Jiefangbei Liberation Monument

Chongqing's Times Square — a 27-metre war memorial tower surrounded by neon-lit pedestrian streets humming until midnight. Street food, crowds, energy. Flat pedestrian zone — good for mobility.

🏗️Built 1947, commemorating WWII victory
🛍️Flat pedestrian zone — good for mobility

Liziba Monorail Station

A metro line that passes directly through the 6th–8th floors of a residential apartment block. Residents hear trains through their walls. A 13-year-old will talk about this for years. Flat street-level viewing.

🚋Active public Line 2 — trains every ~5 mins
🚶Street-level viewing platform — no climbing

🌊

Chaotianmen

The headland where the Yangtze and Jialing rivers meet in a visible line of two different-coloured waters. One of the great river views in China. Lift access from the dock area.

🌊Two rivers visibly meet — different colours of water
🛗Lift access available from riverside
